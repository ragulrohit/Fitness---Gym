Pricing Questions

Clear answers before you choose your plan.

Can I change plans later?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade your membership at the start of your next billing cycle.

Are there joining fees?

No hidden joining fees. Your selected monthly plan is the core membership cost.

Do plans include classes?

Basic includes one group class per week. Pro and Elite include unlimited group classes.

Can I try the gym first?

Yes. New members can start with a trial visit and talk with a coach before choosing a plan.
